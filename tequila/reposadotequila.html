Tequila

Kereru Classic reposado Tequila 100% agave

Warbler reserve reposado tequila is made from white tequila that is deposited in American white oak and French white oak barrels for a period of at least Six months, in which the temperature and humidity conditions are strictly maintained so that at the end of maturation of tequila, obtain the characteristic aromas and flavors of this highest quality product.

100% agave reposado tequila is produced under strict quality control that guarantees that each batch meets both physicochemical and organoleptic quality specifications. The entire process is personally supervised by the expert Tequila Master Mr. Jorge Martínez Cano, who, with his more than 35 years of experience in the tequila industry, manages to obtain the highest quality products on the international market.

VISUAL CHARACTERISTICS

Very bright transparent straw-colored liquid that when shaking the glass generates many sufficiently thick tears that fall slowly due to the natural essential oils of the agave and the barrels. This reflects the quality and good character of WARBLER 100% agave reposado tequila.

AROMATIC CHARACTERISTICS

Warbler Reserve Reposado 100% agave tequila offers woody aromas, where subtle notes of vanilla, chocolate and nuts prevail, all of them obtained from the well-crafted wood of the barrels where this tequila is matured. All its aromas are very soft, inviting you to taste this very complex, high-quality product.

TASTE CHARACTERISTICS

It is a tequila that tastes like the aromas already described. Very smooth, it offers a very pleasant sweetness to the palate due to its maturation in the barrels, it does not hurt the mouth or the throat. The finish is very long, delicious on the palate. The completeness of this product is manifested in the changes it has in the mouth, since, with each sip, you feel the evolution that evokes incomparable quality and complexity, as it begins with vanilla and chocolate and ends with very pleasant dried fruits.
